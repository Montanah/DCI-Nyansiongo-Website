About Us

Deliverance Church International Nyansiongo is a pentecostal church under the Deliverance Church International Ministries, under the able leadership of Bishop. Dr. Mark Kariuki and the Apostolic team. It was started in the year 2002 by Pst. Mr & Mrs. Barnabas and Margaret Mititi; who have steered it to date by the grace of God. The ministry has grown and led to the birth of three other branches in the South Nyanza East region namely;

  • Deliverance Church International Chepilat
  • Deliverance Church International Manga Scheme
  • Deliverance Church International Chepng'ombe

The church has grown in leaps and bounds and in the process God has raised other pastors to help oversee different ministries in the church. These are;

Pst. Vincent Makori
The Associate pastor

Pst. Dennis Ratemo
Worship/Choir ministry & Sunday School

Apst. Kipkemoi Collins
Intercessory & Youth ministries

Location
